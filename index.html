About Me

I am currently living in the San Francisco Bay Area and working remotely with over 7 years professional working experience as well as a Bachelors degree in design. Coworkers have described me as hardworking and dedicated; someone who is devoted to projects from initial concept to final delivery. I was given the title of "Interactive Media Designer" due to my diverse work experience across a myriad of design disciplines such as graphic design, motion graphics/video production, web design/development, and mobile design/development. Most of my days are spent designing in Photoshop/Illustrator, animating in After Effects/Animate, and coding HTML/CSS/JS. My work has been seen by millions of people every month on the web, at tech events such as CES, ComicCon, E3, Mobile World Congress, and even in Times Square, which is really humbling. I am passionate about my work, and I feel that shows in each and every one of my projects. I am currently freelancing and looking for full-time work.

If you would like to get in touch, feel free to email me and say hi.

Design

I feel that I have a passionate connection to design and problem solving. I love the challenge of connecting people with brands and keeping users engaged. I believe that all good design starts with a solid concept.

Animation

Like design, animation is another way to express yourself, and to connect your brand with its audience. A well thought out animation pops off the screen and delivers a memorable message to the viewer.

Development

I taught myself coding skills to allow me to make my designs come to life in the form of interactive websites and prototypes. From my past experiences, I have found that transforming a static design, to a functional thing is a very rewarding experience.
