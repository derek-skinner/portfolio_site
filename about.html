Hello!

I am a designer living in the San Francisco Bay Area and working remotely. I was given the title of "Interactive Media Designer" due to my diverse work experience across a myriad of design disciplines such as graphic design, web design/development, motion graphics, video production, and mobile design/development. Most of my days are spent designing in Photoshop/Illustrator, animating in After Effects/Animate, and coding HTML/CSS/JS. My work has been seen by millions of people every month on the web, at tech events such as CES, ComicCon, E3, Mobile World Congress, and even in Times Square, which is really humbling. I am passionate about my work, and I feel that shows in each and every one of my projects.

I started designing and teaching myself to code when I was in my teens and absolutely fell in love with it. Once I realized it was my passion, I then decided to make it my career when I attended Expression College for Digital Arts and earned my Bachelors degree in "Motion Graphics & Design". During and after college I freelanced for companies like Lux Design, the National Parks Service, and many smaller companies. After freelancing for almost 3 years, I then transitioned to full time at CBS Interactive as a Designer. I worked there for the next 5 years, then started a contract gig at Demandbase so I could work remotely and have more ownership over my creative projects. After fulfilling my contract with Demandbase, I decided to go back to freelancing remotely.

If you would like to get in touch, feel free to email me and say hi.
